## Bio

I am a PhD student (2020-2025) in the Department of Computer Science, Beihang University now, advised by Prof. [Zhoujun Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=e-4LoEcAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) and Prof. [Dong Xu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=7Hdu5k4AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao).
My research interests include Large Langugae Model, Multimodal-learning and AIOps.

Throughout my undergraduate education, I acquired fundamental research skills under the guidance of Prof. [Jin Xie](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=Q7QqJPEAAAAJ) within the team led by Prof. [Jian Yang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=6CIDtZQAAAAJ). 

I interned at Microsoft Research Asia (working with Dr. [Furu Wei](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=G-V1VpwAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao), Dr. [Dongdong Zhang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=w2qu71oAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao)), BAAI (working with Dr. [Qiwei Ye](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=RJ6SuR8AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao)), and Alibaba Qwen Group (working with Dr. [Chang Zhou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=QeSoG3sAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao)).

Besides statistics, I spend most of my time playing basketball, hiking, playing the guitar, and reading books. Recently, I've been focusing on research in large language models. I'm open to discussions, so feel free to reach out!
